How to Get Invited to the Social Club

F
By the Thiiird Place team·February 2026·4 min read

The Social Club is the inner circle of Thiiird Place — a referral-only tier for members who shape the culture. It's not about status; it's about trust. Everyone inside is there because someone vouched for them, and that one rule keeps the room exactly as good as the people in it.

What it unlocks

  • ✦ Private listening rooms — members-only sessions that never touch the public Watch page.
  • ✦ Exclusive events & pre-sale — invite-only nights and the earliest RSVP window.
  • ✦ The Vault — secret mixes, unreleased sets, and downloads from the library.
  • ✦ Member directory & chat — a private space to find collaborators and talk shop.

How to get in

Get vouched for

Membership is by referral. A current member shares your name and their referral details when you apply.

Submit your application

Fill out the short Social Club form — who you are, what you do, and who referred you. It takes a minute.

Get reviewed personally

Every application is read by hand. If it's a fit, we'll reach out with your invitation.

Who it's for

Artists, selectors, builders, and people who show up for the music and the community. If you're already the person bringing everyone together, you'll feel at home here.

Ready to apply?

Have a referral? Start your application now.
