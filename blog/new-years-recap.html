A Thiiird Place New Year's at the Loft

F
By Feelz·January 2026·5 min read

We've always believed the best way to start a year is in a room full of people you actually want to be around. So we opened the Thiiird Space loft, set up the system, and let the night write itself.

Old friends and new friends, side by side. By midnight it didn't feel like a party so much as a family reunion you didn't know you'd been missing.

The room

Low light, warm sound, just enough space to move. People came in not knowing anyone and left with three new numbers in their phone. That's the whole design — build the environment, and connection takes care of itself.

"Somebody walked in alone at 10 and was closing the place down with new friends at 2. That's the win."

The set

Feelz took it across the map — hip-hop into R&B, dancehall into house, afrobeats to close. A genre-fluid night for a genre-fluid room.

The arc of the night

  1. Warm-up — soulful R&B as the room filled in
  2. Hip-hop pocket — the singalong stretch
  3. Dancehall & afrobeats — peak energy
  4. House — the after-midnight stretch
  5. Slow soulful close — last dance
